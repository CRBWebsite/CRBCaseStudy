History of Catanduanes Reef Break

MEET EZRA

The Majestic, in Ezra’s mind, is one of Catanduanes’ ticket to tourism as a surfing destination in the Philippines. He established the Puraran Surfing School and launched it’s first surf clinic on July 27 and 28, 2013. He established surfing schools in Puraran Surf Beach Resort and Catanduanes Midtown Inn. He saw that the Puraran community will be able to use surfing as a way to have a better life and that it will enable the residents to promote tourism while providing sources of income for them.

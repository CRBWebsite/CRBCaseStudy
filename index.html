The Catanduanes Reef Break Experience

On  May 2014, Ezra’s “home break” in the surfing scene began. Catanduanes Reef Break, the “party” as Genesis recalled, was a three-day
festival. Ezra lined up a surf competition and clinic, a beach party, downhill skate, paddle race, bikini open, and “barakalan” or open market. Genesis was so proud of his brother for creating an event in their hometown that has big impact to both the local community and tourists.

Ten days after the CRB event, Ezra Efondo was pronounced dead on arrival due to heart attack. He was on his way home from a skating session when the incident happened

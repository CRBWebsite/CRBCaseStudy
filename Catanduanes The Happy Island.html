Catanduanes

Catanduanes is situated in the Bicol region, the southern Luzon area of the Philippines. The area, like many of the country’s islands, is comprised of 11 municipalities still lushed with greens due to its mountainous district and areas surrounded by bodies of water. Catanduanes is located within the cyclone belt and is visited by 12-15 typhoons a year. Termed as “Happy Island” and “The Land of the Howling Winds,” the best time to visit Catanduanes is from March to August

F R A M E W O R K

 The 6P's Framework for Event Marketing by C.A. Preston

The Six P’s framework for event marketing by Preston (2012) was used as a guide to identify the factors contributing to the success of a destination music festival. The six P’s of event marketing are identified as process, place, product, promotion, price and people

Place

The Place is where all the actions take place. In a destination music festival, it is considered an essential factor where tourism industry may be developed. It is a critical condition that affects the decision-making process of a participant – whether they will attend a music festival or not. Organizers must consider the structural landscape, physical and natural attributes, as well as, infrastructural developments in a location to ensure the success of a festival

Process

The Process refers to the quality of the procedures to which tourism products and services were subjected to. It includes the steps of getting the products up to the delivery to the customers. Contingent plans must be present to guarantee the success of a music festival by ensuring that all P’s in event marketing are accounted for

Product

The Product is the final output offered to the customers. This is defined as the end benefit of the customers given by the organizers. In a destination music festival such as Catanduanes Reef Break, the products pertain to the program flow, the line-up of artists, the surfing and downhill skateboarding competitions

Price

The Price is the determinant of the value of an event. It dictates the success of an event, whether it will be patronized by prospective customers or not. Most destination music festivals provide group rates and packages to encourage increased number of participants

Promotion

The Promotion is identified as the process of marketing and distributing pieces of information to a specific target market through a variety of channels such as radio, newspapers, television, and online platforms. Proper marketing and promotion strategies must be uesed to ensure that the specified target market is captured

People

The People refers to all stakeholders involved in a destination music festival –the organizers, local government actors, tourists, local community members, etc. People serve an important role of serving as the “face” of the event

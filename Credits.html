Ms. Lysa J. Sanchez

Ms. Sanchez is a Part-time Lecturer at the Marketing Management Department of De La Salle University, a Travel Consultant at LJS Pleasures Travel & Tour Services, and a Sales and Management Consultant for the Philippine Center For Environmental Protection And Sustainable Development, Inc (PCEPSDI). She has proven her ability to provide consultancy and research services through her meaningful engagements with a range of institutions, organizations, and private businesses specializing on themes related to sales and marketing, travel and tourism, events management, and business development. Through her expertise, she became a sought-after speaker for several television and radio guestings. She obtained her degree in Master of Marketing Communications at De La Salle University

 Ms. Jhona S. Camba

Ms. Camba is the Program Development and Marketing Specialist in External Relations and Internationalization Office (ERIO) of De La Salle University. Prior to her current role, she served as the Marketing Associate for Inkwise, Administrative Assistant and Event Coordinator for Asia Pacific Business Innovation and Technology Management Society (APBITM), Asia Pacific Roundtable Consumption and Production (APRSCP) and the Center for Engineering Sustainable and Sustainable Development Research (DLSU-CESDR), and Research Assistant for Cleaner Production Project under the United Nations

Ms. Geraldine Pascual

Ms. Pascual is the Operations Manager for direct marketing in a multinational financial protection company and a Part-time Lecturer at the Far Eastern University, Makati. She holds a degree on Master in Marketing Communication. She specializes on the disciplines of strategic marketing, market research and insight tools, and data marketing analytics (platforms and modeling).
